Welcome to my academic webpage.

I am a Senior Lecturer in Banking and Course Director of the MSc in Banking and International Finance at Bayes Business School (formerly Cass), City St George's, University of London.

My research focuses on banking regulation, macroprudential and monetary policy, and mortgage markets. [Research statement]
